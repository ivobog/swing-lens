Help

Daily workflow, button meanings, decisions, warnings, and exports.

Start on dashboard
  1. Upload the daily TradingView screener CSV.
  2. Open the run and preview the IB fetch plan.
  3. Fetch read-only IB bars for tickers and benchmarks.
  4. Refresh the cockpit after OHLCV data is ready.
  5. Review decisions, warnings, incomplete rows, and score breakdowns.
  6. Open charts externally or export CSVs for research notes.

Button Glossary

Preview fetch plan
Shows which ticker/data requests would run before contacting IB.
Fetch IB bars
Queues read-only historical market-data requests.
Refresh cockpit
Scores cached technical data and rebuilds combined decisions.
Exports
Downloads combined, fundamentals, technicals, raw rows, plan, or fetch results.

Decision Labels

Strong candidate
High combined score with complete data.
Candidate
Worth reviewing, but less compelling than the strongest group.
Watchlist
Interesting but not yet strong enough for priority review.
Avoid
Weak, risky, or structurally unattractive for the model.
Incomplete data
Missing fundamentals, technicals, or benchmark context.

Warning Glossary

Warnings are research prompts, not trading instructions.

No technicals No benchmark Short history Negative FCF High leverage Weak liquidity Extreme valuation Dilution Accrual risk Cash conversion Forward weak Dividend payout Sparse data

Safety Notes

SwingLens exposes local research workflows only. It does not place broker orders.
